A Piece of Broken Mirror Suspended on a String

2014

Performance — environment

Conceived and presented with Maximilian Le Cain —Operation Rewrite project

2 palm trees, sheets of paper, tennis racquet, fisherman’s thread, white overalls, foil mask, portable screen, metal sheet, red torch, film, 2 16mm film projectors, recorded sounds

Anagrams by Javi Álvarez

An exploration of failure in language and in projection conceived and presented with Maximilian Le Cain taking as a starting point Witch's Cradle, the unfinished film by Maya Deren and Marcel Duchamp
