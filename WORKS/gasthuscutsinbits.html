The Gas Thus Cuts In Bits

2012 — 13

S16mm and 16mm film, colour and b&w, optical sound, 7:06mins


An ephemental film designed for self-destruction

«To make one see the molecular intervals,
the holes in sounds and forms.»
(Gilles Deleuze, Cinema 2)

An exploration of film's unique performativity and instability where cinema is literally derailed and subjected to stitching.
